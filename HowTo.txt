The PowerShell Script labeled "Card-Cutter.ps1" will automatically cut the card.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pre-requisites:

1) Directory on the Desktop labeled: "<Integer Value> 'CRD' "
2) PCMCIA Card Inserted

-------------------------------------------------------------

Process:
-------

1) Insert Card
2) Navigate to Card Cutter directory on the Desktop
3) Right Click "Card-Cutter.ps1"
4) Select "Run with Powershell"
